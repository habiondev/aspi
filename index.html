Who We Are

ASPI Consulting Engineers, Inc. is a global engineering consultancy based in Baku, Azerbaijan. For years, we have supported governments, financial institutions, and private sector organizations in designing and implementing development projects across a wide range of fields. We combine engineering, management, innovation, and environmental standards to create solutions that deliver measurable value.

With over 25 years of experience, we combine our expertise with key partners in the Republic of Azerbaijan and other former Soviet republics to deliver world-class engineering solutions that meet international standards and regulations.
