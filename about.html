About ASPI

International Expertise. Reliable Partnerships. Sustainable Impact

ASPI Consulting Engineers, Inc. is a global engineering consultancy based in Baku, Azerbaijan. For years, we have supported governments, financial institutions, and private sector organizations in designing and implementing development projects across energy and industrial development, urban planning, water and environmental management, agriculture development and investments, etc..

We combine engineering, management, innovation, and environmental standards to create solutions that deliver measurable value.

ASPI is built on:
  • A diverse team of international experts with multidisciplinary backgrounds
  • Long-term professional relationships with clients, partners, and development institutions
  • Commitment to global standards of quality, sustainability, and ethical practice
  • Proven experience in implementing large-scale and complex international projects
Our core principles: Innovation, Quality, Sustainability!
